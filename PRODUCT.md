# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Immigrants and international professionals seeking to move to or work in Italy. They are navigating complex, bureaucratic credential recognition processes (like CIMEA) and need clear, trustworthy guidance to get their foreign degrees, diplomas, and licenses recognized so they can secure jobs.

## Product Purpose
AVANZA serves as an authoritative, high-conversion landing platform. Its purpose is to demystify Italy's degree recognition system, build immediate trust through founder-led expertise, and convert visitors into actionable leads via a guided Quiz and booked consultations.

## Brand Personality
Professional, empathetic, authoritative, and distinctly Italian. It should feel like a premium concierge service rather than a sterile government portal.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS startup templates that feel disconnected from human services.
- Cluttered, bureaucratic government websites (e.g., standard immigration portals) that increase cognitive load and anxiety.

## Design Principles
- **Clarity over cleverness:** The immigration process is confusing enough; the UI must be exceptionally clear and reassuring.
- **Show the humans:** Emphasize personal connections, leveraging the founders' firsthand experience (e.g., prominent profile cards).
- **Bold, localized identity:** Lean into the Italian flag colors (Green #009246, Red #CE2B37) thoughtfully to anchor the service to its destination.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast for legibility, recognizing users may be stressed or translating content on the fly.
- Clear, descriptive form labels and error states.
- Support for multiple languages (IT, EN, ES, AR, HI, FR, BN) seamlessly integrated into the UI.
